Colofilter.css

Those colorful filters uses CSS blend modes and were heavily inspired by Spotify's new 2015 ads

This technic is called "Duotone", and today, with the power of CSS we can play around with some blend-mode properties to get some cool visual results.

Close Spotify examples

To use these filters, download the colofilter.css file, and add it to the head of your document using:

<link href="path/to/colofilter.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Then, to use the filters, use the proper classes on your elements, and that's it!

<div class="blend-blue">
    <img src="image.png"/>
    <span>lorem</span>
</div>

Unfortunately, colofilter.css will not work with IE, Edge, Opera Mini, or Safari (desktop & iOS), as they don't support the hue, saturation, color, and luminosity blend modes.

Check compatibility for mixblendmode and filters.
